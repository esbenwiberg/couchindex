# Product principles

1. **Continue Watching is first.** No advertisement, hero banner or recommendation row appears above it.
2. **Discovery beats promotion.** Every row must be useful to the user.
3. **Provider agnostic.** Ranking is never influenced by provider preference.
4. **Local-first.** Cached data and local state keep navigation fast and resilient.
5. **One search.** Searching never requires choosing a provider first.
6. **One browse experience.** The user browses CouchIndex, not eight separate catalogues.
7. **Providers own playback.** CouchIndex launches provider apps rather than proxying video.
8. **TV-first.** Navigation, focus behavior and information density are designed for a remote and viewing distance.
9. **Ratings stay transparent.** Sources, scales and vote counts remain distinct.
10. **Complexity must be earned.** No backend, abstraction or platform expansion without a concrete need.
