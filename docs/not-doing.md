# Explicit non-goals

CouchIndex intentionally does not attempt to build:

- A universal video player for commercial streaming services
- DRM circumvention or stream extraction
- Storage of provider usernames, passwords or session tokens
- Browser scraping as a core integration strategy
- A fake combined rating that hides source differences
- A backend before synchronization or multi-device needs justify it
- Mobile and TV abstractions before the TV product is proven
- Provider-sponsored ranking
- Perfect cross-device playback synchronization in V1

These boundaries protect product focus, reliability and maintainability. They may only change through an explicit architectural decision.
