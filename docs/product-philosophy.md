# Product philosophy

Streaming services optimize for engagement. CouchIndex optimizes for decision quality and speed.

The interface should help the user understand what is available, whether it is worth watching and where it can be played. Nothing exists merely to increase time spent in the app.

## Design stance

- No hero advertisements above useful content.
- No sponsored ordering.
- No provider receives preferential treatment.
- Missing data is shown honestly rather than fabricated.
- Fewer remote clicks are better than decorative complexity.
- The app should remain useful when external services are slow or temporarily unavailable.

## Product character

CouchIndex should feel calm, direct and trustworthy. It is a personal index and launcher, not another streaming storefront.
