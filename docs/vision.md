# Vision

CouchIndex is a personal Google TV application that combines the catalogues of subscribed streaming services into one useful browsing experience.

Streaming apps are designed to promote their own content and maximize engagement. CouchIndex is designed to answer a simpler question: **what is worth watching tonight?**

## Product promise

- Search once across all configured services.
- Browse the combined catalogue instead of opening each provider.
- Put Continue Watching first.
- Show ratings and availability without promotional ranking.
- Launch playback in the installed provider application.

## Success criteria

CouchIndex succeeds when it reduces the time and remote clicks required to find and resume content. A useful V1 should make browsing, comparing and launching titles meaningfully better than traversing individual streaming apps.

## Primary user

The initial product is intentionally optimized for one technically capable user, one Google TV environment and a known set of subscriptions. Multi-user, commercial distribution and cross-device synchronization are future possibilities, not V1 requirements.

## Scope boundary

CouchIndex owns discovery, watchlist state and its own Continue Watching model. Streaming providers continue to own authentication, DRM, playback, subtitles and exact playback position.
